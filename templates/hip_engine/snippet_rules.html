The game.

1. Create a mixtape with a few tracks you like, taken from YouTube or SoundCloud.
2. Ask friends to add tracks to your mixtape.
3. Select the tracks you want to keep in your mixtape.
4. Your friends get points for each track you keep.

More specifically...

    • Links from SoundCloud and YouTube ONLY.
    • Five spots for contributors. Empty spots are open to everyone.
    • Contributors have 1 week to add tracks.
    • 3 tracks tops each (mixtape owner and contributors).
    • 1 kept track = 10 pts, 1 like = 3 pts.
