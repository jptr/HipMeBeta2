It's all just a game.

Here is the gist of it.

1 - Start a mixtape with a few tracks you like, taken from YouTube or SoundCloud.
2 - Ask friends to fill in the mixtape with other tracks.
3 - You select the tracks you want to keep in your playlist.
4 - Each track you keep gets points to the contributor. Easy, right?

You get a great mixtape, handcrafted by your friends.
They know you well, they know best what you like.

They get public recognition as great music advisors.
And special badges such as deep-house emperor or post-rock starfighter for everyone to see (coming soon).

Now sign up and enjoy. You won't regret it.
